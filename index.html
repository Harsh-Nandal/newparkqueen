The Story of Our City

Rohtak — named after Raja Rohtash, in whose day the city is said to have been built.

A district in the Indian state of Haryana, traditionally it is also claimed that the town derives its name from the Roherra — the Tacoma undulata tree — called Rohitaka in Sanskrit.

A heaven of calm and serenity amid the bustling entertainment hub of Rohtak — ParkQueen stands justaposed against modern designs and patterns, synonymous with the words comfort, grandeur, and excellence.
